Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on the Signup option in the menu, fill in your details, and you're ready to go!
How do I bookmark articles?
Click the bookmark icon on any article card or on the article page to save it. Your bookmarks appear in the sidebar.
How do I change to dark mode?
Click the moon/sun icon in the top right corner to toggle between light and dark mode.
How do I search for news?
Use the search bar in the navbar to search for articles by title, description, or author.
